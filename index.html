About Us

Tech Syndicate is the prestigious tech club of Amity International School, Sector 46, Gurgaon.

Since its inception in 2016, the Syndicate is constantly working to make information technology accessible to everyone and spark an interest in the youth to keep up with the rapidly progressing industry of computer science.

Robotronics, our annual tech symposium, was first hosted in 2013. With more than 10 events and 250+ participants, this contest has been the much-anticipated challenge of all the tech wizards across Delhi NCR.

Ideatech (Idea Pitching)

Eligibility: IX-XII
1 Team of 3 Members per School

THEME: Bringing a positive change in the Sustainability Spectrum
Sub topics: • Climate Change, responsible consumption and production with special emphasis on sustainable cities and communities.
• Breaking the barriers: Gender equality and Discrimination
• Fundamentals: Quality Education / Healthcare / Food Security / Agriculture

Design experiments in various parameters and carry them out to prove the hypothesis.
Students must develop a product and promote it in any form - be it a game / a mobile application (iOS / Android) / Website / PowerPoint / Movie / 3D model or a combination of all -- and pitch it.

Email us your YouTube video link / PowerPoint Presentation / APK or IPA / images (containing the introduction to your idea) as well as your source code (through GitHub or Google Drive) latest by 5th July 2019 at cyberamity46@gmail.com

Jr Robotrix (Robotics Juniors)

Eligibility: IV-VII
1 Team of maximum 3 Members per School

THEME: SMART CITIES
The objective of the Smart Cities is to promote cities that provide core infrastructure and give a decent quality of life to its citizens, with a clean and sustainable environment and application of ‘smart’ solutions.
In this, Waste Management is an important factor. It involves collection, transportation and disposal of waste.

The Jr Robotrix Challenge is to design a robot which will collect the waste based upon their type (Wet or solid) from homes and transport it to its designated area.

Teams can use any ready-to-assemble kit/ LEGO® MINDSTORM® kit /embedded systems kit available in the market.
For rules and regulations kindly visit: https://bit.ly/2H1bZKE

Vivacity (Flash Movie)
